# Acceptance Criteria

## Dashboard

- Dashboard shows summary cards and visual overview data.
- Dashboard exposes bill alerts through the alert icon.
- Dashboard respects saved theme and formatting preferences.

## Bills

- A user can add, edit, pay, and delete bills.
- Paying a bill moves its paid state into history-related views.
- Bills can be searched and filtered.

## Payment History

- Paid bills appear in a read-only history view.
- Payment History shows final paid totals without double-counting late fees.
- Payment rows remain simple and do not require expansion to understand core information.

## Recurring Bills

- A user can add, edit, delete, and generate recurring bills.
- Recurring bill categories stay aligned with bill-type categories.
- The recurring list shows next bill dates and status clearly.

## Expenses

- A user can add, edit, and delete expenses.
- Expense totals and category summaries update from stored expense records.
- The page supports category filtering and search.

## Budgeting

- A user can add, edit, and delete budget limits by category.
- Budgeting distinguishes bill-based categories from expense-based categories.
- Budget List supports search and filters.
- Budget by Category gives a simple category summary comparable to the Expenses page style.

## Settings

- Account information can be saved.
- Password can be changed with validation.
- Preferences can be saved and reflected in app behavior.
- Dark mode applies across the app.
- Logout and Delete Account are available from Account Actions.
