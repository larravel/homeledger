# Final Requirements

## Final Scope

The final submission scope is a household finance workflow with these primary modules:

1. Dashboard
2. Bills
3. Payment History
4. Recurring Bills
5. Expenses
6. Budgeting
7. Settings

## In-Scope Functional Requirements

- Users can create, edit, pay, and delete one-time bills.
- Users can review paid bills in Payment History.
- Users can create recurring bills and generate due bills from them.
- Users can create, edit, and delete expense entries.
- Users can create, edit, and delete budget limits by category.
- Users can review dashboard summaries for bills, expenses, and payment trends.
- Users can change account information, password, theme, date format, decimal places, and currency preference.
- Users can delete their account from Settings.

## In-Scope UI Requirements

- Main pages use a consistent modern finance-style UI.
- Each main page is readable in both light mode and dark mode.
- Tables and list views support scanning and common actions.
- Important pages provide search and/or filtering where appropriate.

## Non-Goals

- Multi-user collaboration
- Bank API integration
- Real-time push notifications
- Full production-grade reporting/export suite
- Pixel-perfect clone of a third-party product

## Quality Requirements

- The final app on `main` must be coherent and navigable.
- The repository must show reviewable evidence of scope, implementation, and validation.
- Validation commands must be repeatable by the reviewer.
