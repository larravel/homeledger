# **HomeLedger – Household Billing & Expense Manager**

*(Personal / Family Finance Focus)*

---

## 1. Project Overview

HomeLedger is a web-based Household Billing and Expense Management System designed to help families and individuals efficiently manage recurring household expenses and utility payments.

The system focuses on organizing monthly bills, tracking due dates, recording payments, and analyzing household spending patterns. It provides a structured way for users to monitor their financial obligations and avoid late payments through automated reminders and clear payment records.

By digitizing household bill management, HomeLedger allows users to maintain organized financial records, track payment history, and visualize spending trends through simple dashboards and reports.

The system emphasizes simplicity, practical household finance management, and real-world billing scenarios.

---

## 2. Project Objectives

* To help households organize and manage recurring monthly bills
* To track payment status and due dates of utility expenses
* To reduce the risk of late payments through alerts and reminders
* To record payment history for all household bills
* To analyze household spending patterns through visual reports
* To apply financial tracking logic in a web-based application

---

## 3. Target Users

* Families managing household expenses
* Working adults paying monthly utilities
* Renters responsible for bill payments
* Parents organizing family financial obligations

---

## 4. Scope of the System

The system manages the following household billing activities:

* Monthly bill tracking
* Payment recording
* Due date monitoring
* Recurring bill generation
* Utility consumption tracking
* Household expense analysis

The system is designed for personal or family use and operates as a standalone web application that stores and organizes billing records for a household.

---

# 5. Core Features (Household Billing Foundation)

---

## 5.1 Bill Management

The system allows users to create and maintain records of all household bills.

Features include:

* Add new bills
* Edit existing bill details
* Delete bills
* Assign bill category (utility, rent, subscription, loan, insurance)
* Record bill provider
* Set bill amount and due date
* Track payment status (paid, unpaid, overdue)

The system maintains an updated list of all active and past bills for the household.

---

## 5.2 Payment Recording

The system records payments made for household bills.

Features include:

* Record payment date
* Record amount paid
* Automatically update bill status to “Paid”
* Store payment history
* Apply late fee if payment is made after the due date

All payment transactions are saved for reference and future tracking.

---

## 5.3 Monthly Summary Dashboard

The dashboard provides a clear overview of the household’s financial obligations.

Displayed information includes:

* Total Bills for the Current Month
* Total Amount Paid
* Total Unpaid Bills
* Bills Due Within the Next 7 Days
* Overdue Bills

This dashboard allows users to quickly see which bills require immediate attention.

---

# 6. Unique Features (Household-Specific Enhancements)

---

## 6.1 Recurring Bills Automation

The system automatically generates future bills for recurring expenses.

Features include:

* Set recurring frequency (monthly or quarterly)
* Automatically generate the next billing cycle
* Carry forward bill details such as provider, category, and amount
* Maintain a record of recurring billing history

This feature reduces the need to manually create bills each month.

---

## 6.2 Due Date Alert System

The system monitors bill deadlines and provides reminders to users.

Features include:

* Highlight bills that are overdue
* Show warning alerts for bills due within 3 days
* Display alerts on the dashboard
* Label urgent bills clearly in the bill list

This helps households avoid missed payments and late penalties.

---

## 6.3 Utility Consumption Tracker

The system allows users to record and monitor utility usage.

Features include:

* Record monthly electricity or water consumption
* Store usage values (e.g., kWh or cubic meters)
* Display usage history
* Generate graphs showing consumption trends over time

This allows households to monitor how their utility usage changes each month.

---

## 6.4 Late Fee Calculator

The system automatically computes late fees when bills are paid after their due date.

Features include:

* Detect overdue payments
* Apply a late fee based on a fixed percentage
* Automatically calculate additional payment charges

Example computation:

Late Fee = Bill Amount × 5%

This ensures that total payments reflect realistic billing penalties.

---

## 6.5 Household Spending Breakdown

The system analyzes bill data to show how household expenses are distributed.

Features include:

* Pie chart of household expenses by category
* Percentage distribution of spending
* Visual comparison of different bill types

Example categories include:

* Utilities
* Rent
* Subscriptions
* Loans
* Insurance

This helps households understand where most of their money is spent.

Reports help users analyze financial patterns and manage household expenses more effectively.
